Membership Levels

Non Profit Membership Level

Perfect for organizations dedicated to making a difference

This free or low-cost membership is designed for non-profits looking to connect with a broader audience. Benefits include:

  • ✅ Access to exclusive community events
  • ✅ Basic promotional opportunities
  • ✅ Discounts on select training sessions

Bronze Membership Level

A great starting point for growing businesses

Gain visibility and access valuable resources to help your business thrive. Benefits include:

  • ⭐ All the benefits of the "Non Profit" membership
  • ⭐ Featured listing in the member directory
  • ⭐ Invitations to networking events
  • ⭐ Exclusive member discounts on events and training

Silver Membership Level

Enhanced exposure and business growth opportunities

Take your business to the next level with premium benefits that increase visibility and engagement. Benefits include:

  • 🚀 All the benefits of the "Non Profit" and "Bronze" membership
  • 🚀 Spotlight positions on the home page
  • 🚀 Discounted advertising opportunities
  • 🚀 Priority access to special events and training

Gold Membership Level

Maximum exposure and premium perks for top-tier businesses

Our most comprehensive membership offers unmatched benefits for those who want to stand out. Benefits include:

  • 🏆 All the benefits of the "Non Profit", "Bronze" and "Silver" membership
  • 🏆 Exclusive homepage spotlight placement
  • 🏆 Free or heavily discounted advertising packages
  • 🏆 VIP access to events and premium training sessions
  • 🏆 Personalized promotional support
